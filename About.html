Our Story

Beautiful God Ministry International was founded in 2005 with a vision to create a place of worship where people from all walks of life could encounter the transformative power of God's love. What began as a small gathering in a living room has grown into a vibrant community impacting lives across Nigeria and beyond.

Our journey has been marked by God's faithfulness through every season. From our humble beginnings to establishing multiple campuses, we've remained committed to our core mission: to lead people into a growing relationship with Jesus Christ through authentic worship, biblical teaching, and compassionate service.

Our Beliefs

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God and the final authority for faith and life. All scripture is given by inspiration of God and is profitable for doctrine, reproof, correction, and instruction in righteousness.

The Trinity

We believe in one God eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. Each person of the Trinity is fully God, equal in power and glory, executing distinct but harmonious offices in creation and redemption.

Salvation

We believe salvation is by grace alone through faith in Jesus Christ. No one can earn salvation through good works; it is a free gift of God received by confessing with your mouth that Jesus is Lord and believing in your heart that God raised Him from the dead.

The Church

We believe the Church is the body of Christ on earth, empowered by the Holy Spirit to continue Jesus' mission of seeking and saving the lost. The local church exists to worship God, edify believers, and evangelize the world.

Our History

2005

Beautiful God Ministry was founded by Pastor Moses Onyebuchi Okoh with just 12 members meeting in a small apartment in Lagos.

2008

Purchased our first property and built the initial sanctuary with seating capacity for 300 worshippers.

2012

Launched our first outreach program, feeding and clothing over 500 families in the local community.

2015

Expanded to our current location with a new sanctuary seating 1,500 people and launched our first branch church.

2020

Established Beautiful God Ministry International with affiliate churches in three countries and launched our online ministry platform.

Our Mission

To glorify God by making disciples of all nations through the proclamation of the gospel, the teaching of God's Word, and the demonstration of Christ's love.

Our Vision

To be a multicultural community of believers who worship passionately, serve selflessly, and reach our world with the transforming message of Jesus Christ.
